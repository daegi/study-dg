All Eyes on Ahn as He Launches Pres. Campaign
Attention is being drawn to the tasks that Seoul National University Professor Ahn Cheol-soo faces following the announcement of his presidential bid on Wednesday. For Ahn, who has no political experience,
questions are also being raised as to whether he will be able to
complete the elections without being backed by a political party and whether
he will be able to unveil substantial visions.
